Classic Cheesecake Recipe

Look no further for a creamy and ultra smooth classic cheesecake recipe! Paired with a buttery graham cracker crust, no one can deny its simple decadence. For the best results, bake in a water bath.

Ingredients

Graham Cracker Crush

1 and 1/2 cups (150 g) graham cracker crumbs (about 10 full sheet graham crackers)

5 Tablespoons (70 g) unsalted butter, meled

1/4 cup (50 g) granulated sugar

Cheesecake

four 8-ounce blocks (904g) full-fat cream cheesesoftened to room temperature

1 cup (200 g) granulated sugarat room temperature

1 cup (240g) full-fat) sour cream

1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice (optional, but recommended)

3 large eggs at room temperature
